# CYBERTUNE 

CyberTune: Precision Audio Control for Linux

Elevate your listening experience with CyberTune, a sleek and powerful audio equalizer designed specifically for the Linux desktop. Whether you are a casual listener, a gamer, or a professional audiophile, CyberTune gives you the tools to shape your sound exactly how you want it.

Key Features:

Multi-Band Precision: Fine-tune specific frequencies with a high-fidelity equalizer engine.

Modern Interface: A clean, "cyberpunk-inspired" UI that looks great on any desktop environment.

Custom Presets: Save and switch between profiles for different headphones, speakers, or music genres.

Low Latency: Optimized for performance to ensure your audio stays perfectly in sync.

System-Wide Integration: Works seamlessly with your existing Linux audio backend (PulseAudio/PipeWire).
